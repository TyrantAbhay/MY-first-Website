History

Gandhi Graameen Gurukula is putting continuous effort from past three decades to transform the dream of Mahatma Gandhi into reality. The life style of Mahatma is adopted and followed in every activity of students

In 1969 Shri Gudleppa Hallikeri, the great follower of Gandhi and freedom fighter thought to start Gurukula in Hosaratti village of Haveri district. Gudleppa Hallikeri and S. R. Kanthi(The education minister at that time ) together prepared a plan and handed over it to M. G. Handaral, a specialist in education system. M. G Handaral took the responsibility and visited Sabaramati Ashram and given life to the complete plan.

  • Giving life to Gandhi's thoughts, his vision towards education is the primary focus of Gurukula.
  • Students do follow the life style of Gandhi right from the start of the day
